About us
Fandridis Electric story

Fandridis electric was founded in 1986 by mr. Fandridis Manolis. It started as a small team of electricians working with electrical installations in small projects.

Since then, with hard work and dedication Fandridis Electric have grown into one of the most comprehensive companies in the field, offering a wide range of quality services in electrical installations, automation and security systems throughout Greece. Meanwhile, Manolis Fandridis became the President of SEHEA (Association of Electricians Contractors in Attica).

The company strives to keep up-to-date with the latest developments in the field to offer solutions that take advantage of all the newest technologies.
